Quick 15-Minute Routine (No Equipment)

5 exercises • 3 rounds • 15 minutes

  1. Jumping Jacks — 45 seconds, rest 15s
  2. Bodyweight Squats — 45 seconds
  3. Push-ups (knee or full) — 30 seconds
  4. Plank — 40 seconds
  5. Walking Lunges (in place) — 45 seconds

Repeat 3 rounds. Focus on form rather than speed.
